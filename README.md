# ARVeda: Revolutionizing Herbal Education with AI & AR

## **Overview**
ARVeda brings Ayurveda into the modern world using AI and AR. The app offers an interactive way to learn about herbs and remedies, making Ayurveda accessible and fun.

## **Key Features**
- **Herb Recognition**: Scan plants to get Ayurvedic information.
- **Ayurveda Marketplace**: Discover verified herbal products.
- **Gamified Learning**: Engage with quizzes and challenges.
- **3D Herb Visualization**: Explore herbs in 3D.
- **AR Visualization**: Place herbs in the real world using AR.
- **Voice Support**: Multi-language and text-to-speech features.
- **Portability**: Works on all mobile devices.

---

## **Tech Stack**
- **Frontend**: React Native
- **Backend**: Node.js
- **Database**: MongoDB
- **3D Models**: Three.js

---

## **Target Audience**
- **Students**: Botany, Ayurveda, and pharmacy programs.
- **Health-Conscious Users**: Wellness enthusiasts.
- **Vendors**: Local herbal sellers.
- **Ayurveda Promoters**: Government and NGOs.
- **Researchers**: Pharma and Ayurveda researchers.

---

## **Conclusion**
ARVeda offers an innovative approach to learning Ayurveda using modern technology. It helps preserve and share this ancient knowledge in a fun and engaging way.
